Jean-Michel Cousteau Online Adventures

Jean-Michel Cousteau is the son of world famous oceanographer and explorer, Jacques Cousteau. Following in his father's footsteps, Jean developed a new television series about exploring the world's oceans, and learning how to perserve their precious habitats. I built a series of flash-driven components including an interactive game, animated banner-ads, and interactive webpage interstitials, to promote and extend the conent from this exciting television series. The game was an educational piece about the decline of marine wildlife populations.
