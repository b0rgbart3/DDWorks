LaurelLittleTheatre.com

Laurel Little Theatre is a local theatre in the town of Laurel Mississippi - just outside of New Orleans.

Features

  • rotating carousel of show posters
  • automatically updated content as the season progresses
  • annually updated visual theme
  • annually updated show listings
  • 40 year archive of past show info

Technologies

  • PHP
  • HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript
